CHAPTER ONE
An Introduction
It's often hard to think with the ubiquity of our ever faithful yet enigmatic companions that there was a time in which they simply didn't exist. It's difficult even just to pull back and hold some sort of a perspective where we are able to realise how much they pervade each and every one of us. Computers are very much the brain child of modern society. We hang our hat on it, we look to it as a sign of our advancement in the past and our direction for the future. So for those most passionately curious about the life we have stumbled into, then I find there is no better place to start then with intelligent machines.
